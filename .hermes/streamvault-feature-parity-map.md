## Feature parity result

No productive route was intentionally removed. Removed items are test, debug, generated artifact or orphan asset paths. Product functions were preserved through route compatibility, app shell access or Admin Diagnostics. The highest parity risks are browser specific push behavior, HLS playback edge cases and notification cross channel dedupe with legacy payloads.
